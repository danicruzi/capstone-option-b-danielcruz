\# Project: Datos que Hablan — Financial Wellness of Young Professionals in Latin America



\## Goal

Analyse survey data from 500 young professionals across 6 Latin American countries

(México, Colombia, Argentina, Chile, Perú, Brasil) to produce an executive report

for Futuro Digital LatAm, a nonprofit designing financial literacy programmes.



\## Environment

\- Python 3.x, virtual environment located in venv/

\- Key libraries: pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, scipy



\## Data

\- Raw data: data/latam\_finanzas\_2025.csv (500 rows, 21 columns)

\- Clean data: data/latam\_finanzas\_clean.csv (created in Phase 2 — do not overwrite raw file)



\## Naming Conventions

\- Scripts: scripts/0X\_phase\_name.py (e.g. scripts/01\_explore.py, scripts/02\_clean.py)

\- Charts: charts/0X\_short\_description.png (e.g. charts/01\_income\_by\_country.png)

\- All charts must include: a title, labelled axes, and the source note

&#x20; "Source: Encuesta de Bienestar Financiero LatAm 2025, Futuro Digital LatAm"



\## Report

\- Final report saved as analysis-report.md in the project root

\- Written in English

\- Must follow the required section structure defined in the assignment brief
